The plugin uses MNDWI, robust Otsu thresholding, polygon cleanup, shoreline smoothing, and export-ready outputs to generate shoreline vectors for coastal mapping and change analysis. It is designed for researchers, students, and practitioners working on shoreline delineation, coastal monitoring, and geospatial analysis.
